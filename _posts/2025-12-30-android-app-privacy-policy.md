## Overview
Complete Interval Timer ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

## Data Collection
The App stores the following data **locally on your device only**:
- Workout presets (names, timings, segment configurations)
- App settings and preferences

**We do NOT collect**:
- Personal information
- Location data
- Usage analytics
- Any information that leaves your device

## Data Storage
- All data is stored locally using Android's SharedPreferences
- No data is transmitted to external servers
- No accounts or authentication required

## Data Sharing
We do not share any data with third parties. Period.

## Import/Export
Users can export presets to JSON files for backup or sharing. These files remain under your control.

## Permissions
The app uses the following permissions:
- Storage access (READ/WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE): Only for importing and exporting preset files

## Third-Party Services
The App uses the following Android system services:
- Text-to-Speech (TTS): For voice announcements during workouts
- Wakelock: To prevent screen from dimming during active workouts

No data is sent to third parties.

## Data Deletion
You can delete all your data by:
1. Deleting presets individually in the app
2. Clearing app data in Android Settings
3. Uninstalling the app

## Children's Privacy
The App does not collect any information from users. It is suitable for all ages.

## Changes to This Policy
We will notify users of any material changes by updating this page.
